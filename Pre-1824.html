A Rich and Diverse History

Bagan historical site. (Credit: Author)

Sitting at the crossroads of a number of vibrant Southeast Asian cultures, the history and people of Myanmar are just as diverse and rich as the land they inhabit. One of the most ethnically-diverse countries in the world, the history of Myanmar is the history of hundreds of culturally-distinct ethnic groups. Understanding the complex and varied history of the multitudes of ethnic groups that call Myanmar home is key for understanding current political events.
