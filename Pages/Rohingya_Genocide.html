Rohingya Genocide

National League for Democracy (NLD) supporters in Taunggyi before the 2015 elections. (Credit: Author)

The Tatamadaw, or the Myanmar military, has been waging an ongoing genocide campaign against the Rohingya, a Muslim ethnic group living in Rakhine State. An estimated 24,000 Rohingya have been murdered and thousands of people displaced, with many fleeing to neighboring Bangladesh.
Bangladesh has relocated thousands of Rohingya to a remote island that is often flooded by incoming tide levels, dismissing safety concerns about living conditions.
